What we offer

Potty Training

We train your dog where to go to the toilet. Indoor and outdoor potty spot training to help you avoid messy situations in the house. We also train owners on how to handle such accidents better to avoid pet trauma.

Cost: 20K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training




Book

Obedience Training

Obedience is the foundation of all training. In this course, we focus on Commands as well as leash handling to avoid issues during public walks. Obedience acts as the base on which all other packages are built it is a crucial element.

Cost: 25K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training



Book

Home Defense

Home Defense is a package that focuses mainly on home guarding. In this package, the Dog learns to fend off strangers as well as protect the owner. This is a recommended package for those living in areas with low security as well as police dogs

Cost: 35K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training



Book

Aggression Training

Aggression training is important to either build on the already aggressive dog, control the aggression or help fearful dogs become aggressive. This package paired with obedience training creates a dog who is aggressive and can be controlled by the owner by commands.

Cost: 25K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training

Book

Sniffer Dog Training

Sniffer Training allows dogs to be used in retrieval missions. This package is suitable for hunting dogs as well as police and army sniffer dogs. These dogs can also be used in search and rescue missions in collapsed buildings as well as in calamities

Cost: 30K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training


Book

Anti-poison Training

Anti-poison training helps prevent dog poisoning. In this package, the dog is taught to only accept food from its owner and no one else. By enforcing this rule we avoid the common case of dog poisoning and ensure the preservation of our canine companions

Cost: 25K

Payable in two installments half at the beginning and the rest at the end of Training
