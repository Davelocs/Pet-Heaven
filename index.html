Home Defense is a package that focuses mainly on home guarding. In this package, the Dog learns to fend off strangers as well as protect the owner. This is a recommended package for those living in areas with low security as well as police dogs

Aggression Training

Aggression training is important to either build on the already aggressive dog, control the aggression or help fearful dogs become aggressive. This package paired with obedience training creates a dog who is aggressive and can be controlled by the owner by commands.
